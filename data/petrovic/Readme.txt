'Petrovic' Dataset:

Data from a series of subjective image fusion evaluation trials performed as a part of image fusion research activity at the University of Manchester.

Petrovic V, "Subjective Tests for Image Fusion Evaluation and Objective Performance Metric Validation", Information Fusion, vol. 8, April 2007, pp. 208-216.

doi:10.1016/j.inffus.2005.05.001
